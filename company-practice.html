Free Zone

Free Zone Company Formation

Dubai Free Zones are the top choice for entrepreneurs who want 100% ownership, tax benefits, and fast setup. Free Zone company formation in Dubai allows businesses to enjoy world-class infrastructure and simplified processes without local sponsorship requirements.

  • 100% foreign ownership
  • Zero personal & corporate taxes
  • Easy license & visa process
  • Access to international markets
  • Cost-effective setup
We help you choose the right free zone & handle registration.
Mainland

Mainland Company Formation

If you’re looking to tap into the UAE’s local market, Mainland company formation in Dubai is the ideal choice. Mainland companies allow you to operate without geographical restrictions and work with both private and government entities.

  • Full ownership in many business sectors (new UAE laws)
  • No trade restrictions in UAE
  • Unlimited employee visas
  • Opportunity to bid for government contracts
  • Flexibility to expand across the UAE
Offshore

Offshore Company Formation

For global investors, Offshore company formation in Dubai is an excellent option for asset protection, wealth management, and international trading. Offshore companies provide tax benefits, confidentiality, and no need for a physical office in the UAE.

  • 100% foreign ownership
  • No corporate or income tax
  • Confidential shareholder details
  • No minimum capital requirement
  • Access to UAE’s strong banking network
